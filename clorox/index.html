Context

Berkeley Innovation is a student-run design consultancy. Each semester, consultants tackle a real-world design problem for our clients.

In the spring of 2019, I worked with You Jin Lee, Maria Terrenal, and Cherry Wu to design an internal site for Clorox's Technology Advancement Center, a team that experiments on innovative ideas.

These designs were implemented by a team of Clorox interns, and the site is now live.
