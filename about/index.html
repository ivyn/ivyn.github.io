I'm Ivy, a senior at UC Berkeley finishing up a B.A. in Computer Science and a Certificate in Design Innovation. My education has been built from engineering, the humanities, and business.

I’m a product manager who’s been from A to B to Z in the product lifecycle. I have a bias for impact and experience with communicating across a crossfunctional team.

UC Berkeley has been a place for me to try things.

  • I managed consultants as a project lead at Berkeley Innovation, a student-run human-centered design consultancy.
  • I taught the Intro to Illustrator and Photoshop course in the pursuit of Innovative Design's mission to make Berkeley beautiful and share creative knowledge.
  • As part of The Berkeley Forum, I helped provide a non-partisan forum where people like U.S. presidential candidate Rand Paul, Internet pioneer Vint Cerf, and PayPal founder Peter Thiel could share a wide range of ideas.
